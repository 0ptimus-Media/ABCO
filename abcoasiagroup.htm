ADDICTION

ADDICTION PET FOODS

Sole & Exclusive Distributor Since 2003

When ABCO Asia was founded, Addiction Pet Foods was our first brand partnership—and what a choice it proved to be. Addiction didn't just choose any distributor in Malaysia. They chose us. And they've stayed with us for over 20 years. In a market flooded with options, that's not luck—that's a vote of confidence.

Addiction is the gold standard in holistic pet nutrition, pioneered by a vision that shouldn't exist (but does): a brand that cares as much about your dog's health as a veterinary surgeon would. Born in New Zealand in 2002, Addiction was founded when a visionary clinical nutritionist and veterinary surgeon realized that most pet foods were causing harm rather than helping. His personal motivation? Helping his own dog, Zack, overcome debilitating allergies.

The New Zealand Advantage: New Zealand's remote, pristine environment is free from mad cow and foot-and-mouth disease. The country's free-range, organic-rich farming practices produce meat sources uncontaminated by artificial hormones, antibiotics, or growth stimulants. This is where Addiction sources its proteins—wild venison, grass-fed lamb, sustainably caught fish. Not a single ingredient comes from China; every component is traceable, ethical, and human-grade quality.

The Addiction Difference:

  • Pioneer in Hypoallergenic Nutrition: Addiction was first to recognize that common proteins cause allergies; they revolutionized the industry with novel, digestible proteins
  • Air-Dried Innovation: Creating nutrient-dense, shelf-stable foods that preserve the essence of raw nutrition
  • Science-Backed Formulation: Every recipe is built on 20+ years of research, featuring balanced amino acids, omega ratios, and taurine for heart health
  • Minimal Recalls: In nearly 22 years, only one voluntary recall—a testament to quality obsession

Why ABCO Brought Addiction In: We recognized early that Malaysian pet owners were evolving. They wanted better. They wanted proven. They wanted a brand that stood for something beyond profit. Addiction was that brand. Over 20 years later, it remains our hero offering, trusted by premium boutique pet shops and serious pet enthusiasts across Malaysia.

AIXIA

AIXIA

Japan's Premier Cat Food Legacy – First & Sole Distributor in Southeast Asia

In a region flooded with generic cat foods, ABCO was the first to recognize AIXIA's potential and introduce it to Malaysia. Now it's one of our strongest performers. AIXIA's name itself tells the story: "Ai" (love) + "Xia" (happiness from "shiawase") = "To Love is Happiness." Since 1990, this Japanese institution has focused exclusively on feline nutrition with an attention to detail that borders on obsessive.

The Gold Standard: AIXIA's KIN-CAN is quite literally the gold standard of Japanese cat food. Introduced in 1985 as Japan's first premium mini-can of tuna white meat flakes, KIN-CAN has been Japan's most trusted premium canned cat food for nearly 40 years. Cat owners don't just feed their cats Kin-kan—they choose it, repeatedly, passionately.

Japanese Precision:

  • Human-Grade Quality: Made with the same quality standards as human food; customers have genuinely said the tuna tastes "better than regular canned tuna"
  • Umami Excellence: Deep tuna flavor enhanced with Japanese broth (dashi) and bioavailable iron to prevent feline anemia
  • Innovation Beyond Food: The i CARE series features peptides co-developed with Kitasato University—a breakthrough in feline digestibility and taste
  • Hydration-Focused: Recognizing that cats often don't drink enough water, AIXIA's pastes and broths encourage natural hydration

Why Pet Parents in Malaysia Love It: Japanese pet food is synonymous with meticulous quality control and innovation. AIXIA represents that promise. Whether it's the classic Kuro-can (dark tuna with skipjack) or the hydration-focused i CARE line, AIXIA offers solutions Malaysian cat owners are hungry to find. By being the exclusive distributor, ABCO ensures consistent availability of a brand that's become a status symbol among serious cat enthusiasts.

ZEALANDIA

ZEALANDIA

New Zealand's Ethical, Holistic Innovation

While Addiction pioneered holistic nutrition, Zealandia represents the next evolution: sustainable, ethically sourced, species-aligned feeding. It's what happens when innovation meets conscience. Zealandia was created by visionary founders who asked: "What if we could feed pets the way nature intended—using byproducts that would otherwise be wasted, sourcing animals treated humanely, and creating complete nutrition in a single can?" The answer was Zealandia.

The Sustainability Story:

  • Ethical Sourcing: Every protein source is certified by New Zealand's Ministry of Primary Industries, adhering to stringent animal welfare standards
  • Unique Proteins: Including brushtail possum (invasive species that damages native NZ forests—eating it protects the ecosystem), wild-caught king salmon, grass-fed lamb
  • Green-Lipped Mussels: Natural anti-inflammatory omega-3s from sustainable NZ waters
  • Minimal Waste: Using nutrient-dense organ meats and broths ensures nothing is wasted

Nutritional Innovation:

  • 60% meat protein on a dry-matter basis (extraordinarily high)
  • Natural green tripe for enzymatic support and digestive health
  • Grain-free, filler-free formulations for sensitive pets
  • Life-stage specific nutrition for puppies, adults, seniors

Why ABCO Carries It: Zealandia represents the future of premium pet nutrition—one where ethics and nutrition are inseparable. Malaysian pet owners increasingly demand brands that align with their values. Zealandia delivers that. For boutique retailers positioning themselves as premium, ethical operators, Zealandia is essential inventory.

VERSELE-LAGA

European Mastery Since 1932 – 131+ Years of Family Expertise

Versele-Laga is old Europe doing what old Europe does best: perfecting craft through generations. Founded in 1932 by Prudent Versele in rural Deinze, Belgium, this fourth-generation family business has quietly dominated European pet nutrition for nearly a century. Today, with 11 manufacturing facilities across multiple continents and a presence in 75+ countries, Versele-Laga serves over 15 million pets daily.

The European Legacy:

  • Over 2,100 Products: From specialist bird foods to premium dog and cat nutrition, Versele-Laga is a complete ecosystem
  • Research-Driven: Every formula is backed by nutritional science and rigorous testing
  • Multi-Species Expertise: Unique in the industry—equally excellent at feeding birds, small mammals, dogs, cats, and farm animals
  • Opti Life Premium Range: Their flagship premium lines for dogs and cats represent European standards for grain-free, nutrient-dense formulation

The Versele-Laga Principles:

Why ABCO Represents Versele-Laga: For retailers seeking European credibility and complete product range solutions—from specialty bird foods to premium cat kibble—Versele-Laga is non-negotiable. SUPREME

The UK's Specialist Innovation Leader – 30+ Years of Small Animal Nutrition

While others focused on dogs and cats, Supreme Petfoods saw an underserved market and became the global leader: high-quality, species-specific nutrition for small animals. Founded in 1990 from a rural Suffolk farm, Supreme began by delivering quality hay to local pet shops. The founder recognized that small pets—rabbits, guinea pigs, hamsters, ferrets—were being fed generic, nutrient-poor foods. So he did something radical: he created species-specific, veterinary-recommended diets.

The Supreme Achievement:

  • Science Selective Range: Veterinary-recommended, formulated for specific species at specific life stages
  • Award Recognition: 8 consecutive years of Small Animal Product of the Year; 2024 Manufacturer of the Year
  • Natural Ingredients: No added sugar, no artificial colors—only ethically sourced, traceable components
  • Dedication to Standards: Supreme uses the same standards as human food production; each production line is dedicated (vegetarian food made in vegetarian environment, etc.)

The Tiny Friends Farm & Science Selective Brands:

  • Premium positioning for discerning small animal enthusiasts
  • Tasty yet nutritious—pets actually love it
  • Health-focused formulations for sensitive digestive systems
  • Educational materials helping owners understand proper nutrition

Why ABCO Carries It: Malaysia has a growing small animal pet community—guinea pigs, rabbits, hamsters, ferrets. Supreme is the ONLY brand that takes these pets seriously nutritionally. It's inventory that attracts a specific customer: the owner who views their small pet as family and will pay premium prices for premium nutrition.
